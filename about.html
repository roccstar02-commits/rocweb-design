About Me

Hi, I'm Rocco Loccisano

I graduated from Southern New Hampshire University with a Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, which gave me a strong understanding of technology, security, and how websites should be built properly.

I started RocWeb Design to help local and small businesses get modern, professional websites without huge agency costs. My goal is to make the process simple, clear, and focused on helping businesses look more credible online.

I focus on clean design, mobile-friendly websites, and helping businesses get more customers online. I am easy to work with and keep projects low-stress for business owners who just want a website that looks good and works well.
